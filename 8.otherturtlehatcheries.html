KOSGODA AND BENTOTA


Welcome! Kosgoda and Bentota, two Sri Lankan towns, are located close to one another and are also closer to Hikkaduwa. On this page, you may learn more about the two turtle hatcheries.

Tourists may find a lot to enjoy in Sri Lanka, a country with a rich culture and aesthetic appeal. The nation is a mixing pot of experiences, offering everything from breathtaking animals and distinctive cuisine to beautiful beaches. The numerous turtle hatcheries that are scattered around the coastline are one of the features that make it a popular tourism destination. A special chance to see turtles up close and take part in preservation efforts is offered by the turtle conservation programs in Kosgoda and Bentota, which are particularly remarkable.
