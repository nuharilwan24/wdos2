WELCOME TO THE HIKKADUWA TURTLE HATCHERY


Five different species of sea turtles visit the beaches of Sri Lanka, all of which are in danger of going extinct because of habitat degradation and mounting human pressures. Organizations like the Hikkaduwa Sea Turtle Hatchery try to address these problems and give these aquatic reptiles a chance at life. Turtle hatchlings are released into the ocean when they are ready to hatch from their eggs, while old and damaged turtles are offered sanctuary and rehabilitation here.


Visit the nearby sea turtle hatchery if you're ever in Hikkaduwa; it's a once-in-a-lifetime chance to get up close and personal with these kind marine reptiles and discover how you can help protect these amazing creatures for present and future generations to enjoy.
